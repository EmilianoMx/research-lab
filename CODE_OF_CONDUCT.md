# Code of Conduct

## Purpose
The purpose of this Code of Conduct is to ensure a welcoming and inclusive environment for all participants in our research community. We are committed to fostering a culture of respect, integrity, and professionalism.

## Research Ethics

1. **Integrity:** We uphold the highest standards of integrity in all our research activities. Fabrication, falsification, and plagiarism are strictly prohibited.

2. **Respect for participants:** We prioritize the rights and well-being of research participants. Informed consent must be obtained, and confidentiality ensured.

3. **Responsible publication:** We aim to publish research findings truthfully, and credit must be given to all contributors, including proper citations.

4. **Transparency:** We advocate for transparency in methodologies and findings to promote trust and reproducibility in research.

5. **Collaboration:** We encourage collaborative work and respect for diverse perspectives within our research teams.

## Expected Behavior
- Treat everyone with respect, regardless of their background or expertise.
- Be open to constructive feedback and communicate openly.
- Engage in discussions and debate respectfully.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, bullying, or any form of verbal abuse will not be tolerated.
- Use of inappropriate language or imagery in any communication is unacceptable.

## Consequences of Unacceptable Behavior
Participants found to be in violation of this Code of Conduct may face sanctions ranging from a warning to expulsion from the research community.

## Reporting Violations
If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to the appropriate authorities within the organization.
